Study Area

Kafr El-Sheikh Governorates is one of the governorates in the Nile Delta region of Egypt .

The governorate has a population of approximately 3.1 million people and covers an area of aroud 3.500 aquare kilometers .

The governorates ia divided into 11 administrative districts , and its capital is kafr El-Sheikh .

Topic of Study

Kafr El-Sheikh Governorates, like many other regions In Egypt faces number of health challenges .

The Governorate has a high prevalence of chronic diseases such as diabetes , hypertension , and heart disease , which are major contributors to morbidity and mortality in the region .

The governorate has several public hospitals and health centers that provide a wide range of medical services to its residents .

These facilities are staffed by trained medical professionals who provide quality care to patients

Multi-Criteria Decision Making For Hospital Site Selection In Kafr El-Sheikh Governorate

Health services must be located in locations that conform to the agreed health standards .

This study aims to provide health services to citizens by exploiting the available financial and human resources .

The standards that were used in this study are believed to be acceptable and appropriate in accordance with the social environment and study conditions.

  • To be far from health centers or other hospitals
  • To be in a densely populated area
  • To be in an urban area
  • To be in an area with a flat surface
  • To be close to the train stations
  • To be close to the car parking complex
  • To be far from canals or banks
